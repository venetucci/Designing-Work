Think Deeply About Tech

Designing Work is a newsletter for people in tech who want to dig a little deeper. It’s a response to the go-with-your-gut, structurlessness-as-a-feature, surface-level groupthink that is so often accepted as our objective truth. It’s an open discussion about the reality under which we operate and ideas for designing a better system. For those of us who feel just a little (or a lot) uncomfortable, thrive on thinking deeply, and often feel like there isn’t a place for our voice, this newsletter is for you. 

Curated by Michelle

If there's one thing I've learned from working in tech, it's that the biggest barriers to doing good work tend to fall in the realm of human problems. I've always been interested in the ambiguous challenge of figuring out how to help teams work together and build products that actually make people's lives better. This newsletter is a way for me to use my interdisciplinary background in planning, community organizing, anthropology, design, and project management to open a discussion about how we make decisions.
