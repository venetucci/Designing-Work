What is Designing Work?

Designing Work is a newsletter for people in tech who want to dig a little deeper. It's a response to the go-with-your-gut, structurlessness-as-a-rule, surface-level groupthink that is so often accepted as our objective truth. It's an open discussion about the reality we operate under and ideas for designing a better system. For those of us who feel just a little (or a lot) uncomfortable, thrive on thinking deeply, and often feel like there isn't a place for our voice, this newsletter is for you.
